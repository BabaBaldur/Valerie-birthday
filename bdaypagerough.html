VALHALLA

How does it feel, Tehani, my dearest,
that, akin to the skies above
Your skin teems with manifold constellations
patterns gingerly depicted in symmetry and allure
tattoos of fire?
How does it feel,
that, akin to the stars out in the void,
the world looks up to you
composes a psalm of gratitude and reverence
and bows low to your brilliance?


I am at a loss for words
I look back onto the depictions of old
and I see you just as clearly as I have always been blessed to
beyond my hiatus, I take to the glowing canvas of your skin
and I revel in the glamour and the wonder
and the childlike marvel that consumes me whole
and I refuse to let it go
I am grateful for the archive of words
that I have put up over the years
and I can recite them incessantly
if it were up to me
you'd be a deity in the flesh

Today, in celebratory fashion
I join the universe that orbits your brilliance
and we all stop to peer
and we perceive that
over all creation none resembles the deep dazzling void of space
as you do
and I can, in full confidence
concede that even in quantum superposition
look upon you and your glamour will never drop a shade.
look at you
miniature galaxies gyrate gleefully within the captivating contours of your eyes
harbingers of doom
and just as the archives of my opus reveal,
they reveal too, and they bring forth the prophecy of our doom
and I embrace it in its fullness
but for now, we raise a toast
and we feast in all manner to another year
of the colourful warmth that you radiate
at least, I imagine,
to all that exists

MERIDIUS
I find it fitting that a birthday is a special day that stands out above the rest. It reminds me of the logic I use to conjure this webpage for you. At the top one has their life, and within their life there are other classes like Relationships, marriages, educational achievements, and the list goes on. A wedding anniversary, for example, exists within the bounds of a marriage, which exists within the bounds of life. In one way or another, one bound may fall, another may rise and fall again, but you must keep living and loving.

For as long as we are together, we may celebrate Valentines, our anniversary and all other kinds of days we hold dear, but solely at our discretion. Every day is a new opportunity to love each other with all our hearts, and these days are special in their own way because we choose to circle them in our calendar. Whether we celebrate them or not, we will keep loving each other every waking day, and we are not at a loss. But a birthday exists at the heart of your humanity, and beyond recognising you as a girlfriend, or a mother, a daughter, a sister, we can all acknowledge your humanity and hold it dear.

Today, you turn 20, a testament to brilliance, resilience and experience. By no means has living been easy, and in that manner life becomes of worth. And we can all celebrate that you've made it through 20 years. You have had so much to live for, and out of life you have been blessed, and you have blessed too. We can only hope that your experience has been just as immersive as you have made it for the people around you. We all thank you, and we congratulate you, for an achievement so subtle yet so special as living out 20 years of the human experience. I can only wish you lots more years coupled with glorious experiences and lessons and I hope that at the end of it all, you will have made it out with a canvas of beauty alone.
